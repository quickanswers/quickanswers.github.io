JEE Advanced Chemistry Paper-1 | Question-7 | Detailed Solution

The electrochemical extraction of aluminum from bauxite ore involves the electrolysis of Al2O3 mixed with Na3AlF6 to give Al and CO2. This process is known as the Hall-Héroult process. Here’s a step-by-step explanation of the process: Bauxite ore, which is a mixture of aluminum oxide (Al2O3) and other minerals, is mined from the earth.
